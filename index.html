    • About Us

    It's an association of judges (member of judiciary) of subordinate courts of Bangladesh. This is a well organized association with a goal to acheive the utmost target of the judiciary of our country to confirm the right to access to the justice. The association is also commited to secure the welfare of every member of judiciary and to creat an expart group of judges who will carry the goal and lead the future judiciary of the country.

    Our Activities

    Bangladesh Judicial Service Association arrange Judicial Conference in every year. It also observs every national day with dignity and pride. It has some programe to help the people during any catastrophical situation of Bangladesh.

    Our Members

    At present the association has more two thousand members who are working as judges in subordinate courts and as various officials in deputation. Click here to visit the list of members of the judiciary.
